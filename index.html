Request Flow

The end-to-end journey of a request — from origination to delivery and feedback.

1

Requests Originate

BUs submit feature & ops requests. Separately, the internal DT Team surfaces proactive strategic initiatives.

2

Backlog Intake

Business PO gathers BU requests. App Dev PO gathers both BU feature requests and DT strategic initiatives.

3

Sprint Planning

POs and Champions collaborate to approve and prioritize backlog items together — ensuring business alignment and capacity planning.

4

Finalize Priority

Approved and sequenced work is ready for assignment. App Dev PO balances reactive BU demand against proactive DT projects.

5

Assign to Pod

Work assigned to the right pod based on BU or app domain.

6

Deliver

BA & SA execute; output delivered back to BU or app.

Feedback Loop

Completed work triggers new requests; PO is notified.

Capacity Allocation Strategy

How each PO divides pod capacity into fixed "buckets" so no single type of work dominates.

POs don't just rank requests 1–100. They reserve a percentage of capacity for each category of work — guaranteeing technical health and innovation aren't crowded out by business demand. Percentages are a starting point, tuned each planning cycle.
Business Growth / Pain PointsHigh-impact requests from the 5 Business Units (new apps, workflow fixes)
Technical Enablement / DebtRequests from Dev/Data teams that make future work faster or safer (API cleanup, data ingestion)
Operational SupportKeeping the current dashboards and apps running
Innovation / DiscoveryHigh-risk, high-reward technical ideas that need a "mini-discovery" by the BA/SA pod
